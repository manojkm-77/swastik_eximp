About Us

Who We Are

Swastik Eximp is a Karnataka-based import agency dedicated to redefining how premium edible oils reach India's food industry. Our network of certified overseas refineries, combined with experienced trade professionals, guarantees timely and compliant delivery — regardless of destination.

Every order is backed by a formal quotation and confirmed contract. 10% advance via LC/RTGS, balance on Lorry Receipt — no ambiguity, no last-minute surprises.

All materials must meet agreed specifications. Buyers have full inspection rights at the dispatch point. Any quality deviation may lead to rejection — we hold ourselves to that standard.

We partner with refineries that follow responsible palm cultivation and sunflower processing standards, ensuring our supply chain meets global sustainability benchmarks.

Tax Invoice, E-Way Bill, Lorry Receipt, and Weight Slip provided on every shipment. Fully GST-compliant, audit-ready, and clean at all times.
