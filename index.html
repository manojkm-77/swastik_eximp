About Swastik Eximp

Swastik Eximp is an international export company based in India, delivering premium commodities to buyers worldwide. We combine deep market expertise, rigorous quality control and efficient logistics to serve distributors, wholesalers and retailers across regions.

Product Categories

We export carefully sourced, inspected and packaged commodities that meet international buyer expectations.

Spices

Premium pepper, turmeric, cumin and blended spice mixes — traceable to certified farms with standardized packaging options.

Standards: FSSAI, APEDA, ISO | MOQ: 1 MT | Packaging: 25–50 kg kraft bags

Textiles

Woven and knitted fabrics, finished garments with quality checks and export-compliant labeling.

Standards: OEKO-TEX, ISO | MOQ: 500 pcs | Packaging: palletized bundles

Agricultural Products

Grains, pulses and specialty commodities, processed and packed to meet destination country requirements.

Standards: APEDA, FSSAI, ISO | MOQ: 2 MT | Packaging: bulk bags / consumer packs

Custom export lines on request — food ingredients, herbs and other specialty items with documentation support.
